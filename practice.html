Process

How I validate (6 simple steps)

A practical process that moves you from “idea” to “brand-fit decision” quickly.

1) Keyword opportunities

Identify clusters with intent + stable demand signals.

2) Market overview

Check size, seasonality, pricing ranges, and saturation.

3) Competitor analysis

Find listing weaknesses and differentiation angles.

4) Review mining

Extract pain points, requests, and objections for strategy.

5) Profitability checks

Fees, margins, sourcing logic, and pricing headroom.

6) Final scoring & blueprint

Go/No-Go decision + roadmap and positioning.
